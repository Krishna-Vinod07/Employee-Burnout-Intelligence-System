# Employee Burnout Prediction & Workforce Intelligence System

## Overview

Employee Burnout Prediction & Workforce Intelligence System is an AI-powered workforce wellness platform designed to monitor employee wellbeing, predict burnout risks, generate future burnout forecasts, and provide actionable recommendations for employees and HR teams.

The system combines Machine Learning, AI-generated insights, and workforce analytics to help organizations identify burnout risks before productivity declines.

---

## Features

### Employee Portal

* Mood and wellness tracking
* Stress, energy, sleep, and workload monitoring
* Burnout risk prediction
* AI-generated wellness recommendations
* Productivity and focus analytics
* 6-week burnout forecasting

### HR Portal

* Workforce analytics dashboard
* Employee monitoring system
* High-risk employee identification
* Intervention planning and tracking
* Workforce distribution analysis
* AI-generated workforce reports
* Employee detail intelligence pages

---

## Machine Learning Models

### Burnout Classification

* Algorithm: Random Forest Classifier
* Accuracy: 80.75%
* Multi-class prediction:

  * Low Risk
  * Moderate Risk
  * High Risk
  * Critical Risk

### Burnout Forecasting

* Algorithm: Random Forest Regressor
* MAE: 6.95
* RMSE: 8.53
* R² Score: 0.84
